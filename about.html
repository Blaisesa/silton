About Us

At Silton, we believe everyone deserves a home that grows with them

Our Philosophy – “Start Small and Grow Big”

From compact starter homes to luxury residences and estate designs, we create spaces that fit every lifestyle and budget. Our philosophy, “Start Small and Grow Big,” means you can begin with an affordable, high-quality home and expand it step by step — whenever you’re ready.

What We Offer

  • Modular Homes: Customizable, high-quality homes built in sections for easy expansion.
  • Eco-Friendly Designs: Energy-efficient materials and sustainable building practices to reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Flexible Financing: Affordable payment plans to help you start your home journey stress-free.
  • Expert Support: Dedicated team to guide you through design, construction, and expansion.

We offer flexible, creative solutions for extra space, relaxation, or investment — designed to fit your goals and lifestyle.

How We Work

Our streamlined process makes building and expanding your home simple and enjoyable:

  1. Architectural design and planning: We work with you to create a comprehensive plan that meets your needs and budget.
  2. Building permits and documentation: We handle all necessary permits and paperwork for your project.
  3. Construction and finishing: We build your home in sections at our facility, ensuring quality and efficiency.
  4. Mortgage and financing support: We assist you in finding the right financing options for your new home.
